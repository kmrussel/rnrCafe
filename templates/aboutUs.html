OUR STORY

R&R Restaurant was founded in 2007 by sisters, Ashley and Katie Russell. They aimed to create a restaurant that combines the feeling of hominess and fine dining by taking their family recipes to the next level?(lol). With the help of their parents, R&R restaurant has become the number one place for families to gather in San Diego.

In 2010, the youngest Russell, Lacey Marie, opened up a second branch which is now known as R&R Cafe. R&R Cafe is an innovative brunch destination that serves a variety of unique breakfast and lunch dishes as well as coffee made to order.
